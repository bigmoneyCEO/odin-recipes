Oven Scrambled Eggs

Back to main recipe list!

Description

These light and fluffy scrambled eggs are a snap to put together for a large crowd. Originally by Erin at allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 24 eggs
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 2 1/2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Pour melted butter into a baking dish. Whisk eggs and salt together. Pour egg mixture into baking dish.
  3. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes. Stir, and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Serve immediately
